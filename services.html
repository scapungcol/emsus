Our Services

Methodology

Emerald's Founder and CEO spent two years developing our sustainability methodology specifically designed for small- and medium-sized enterprises. This approach is based on an in-depth case study of your business, pinpointing organizational strengths and opportunities, followed by the creation of a customized strategy for implementation.

Services

In-Depth Case Studies. Through a systematic assessment process, Emerald provides each client with a comprehensive report, including a review of where your organization currently is with its sustainability activities and opportunities for future growth.

Strategy Development and Implementation. Emerald builds a strategy designed with your employees, clients, and business goals in mind. We also provide support in implementation, as well as creating metrics to track and measure your progress.

Green Brand Marketing and Accreditation. Emerald provides marketing strategies and reporting standards to share with stakeholders, as well as guidance on green accreditation processes..
