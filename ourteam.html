Gloria Cavallaro, Copywriter

Gloria Cavallaro received her Bachelor of Arts with High Honors from the Macaulay Honors College at the City University of New York wherein she studied English Literature & Composition and Studio Art.

In 2013, she started her own business providing copy writing, content creation, and freelance creative directing services to tech startups. She draws from her diverse experience in digital media, photography, and tech industries to successfully execute challenging projects that yield dynamic results for her clients.

Gloria's work with Emerald includes copy writing, social media strategy, and client content consulting.

Her work can be seen at www.gloriacavallaro.com.

Sarah Capungcol, Graphic Designer

Sarah Capungcol is a graphic designer with experience designing, developing, wireframing and prototyping designs for print, websites, and mobile applications.

Before she joined Emerald’s team, Sarah graduated from UPenn’s School of Engineering and Applied Science with a BSE in Bioengineering. As an undergraduate, Sarah developed her passion for the graphic arts through various extracurricular outlets.

Sarah's work can be seen at scapungcol.com.

Brooke Sambol, Intern
