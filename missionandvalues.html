Our Mission

At Emerald our mission is to equip organizations with realistic and achievable strategies that make a tangible difference in their sustainability goals. We offer expertise tailored to fit the needs and budgets of each individual entity, focused on identifying cost-savings and increasing profits. Our work is based on a data- and results-driven methodology developed by our Founder and CEO.

Our Values

Customer-Oriented Approach. We work with our clients to achieve lasting sustainability solutions, building projects around their needs and values. The expression “one size fits all” does not exist in our vocabulary.

Transparency and Integrity. We use proven data and a tested methodology. No gimmicks, no games.

Measured Results. Our systematic, metrics-oriented approach is integrated into all of our strategic thinking and decision-making.
